unique team experience

Do you want to try out the tension and fun in the style of the Boyard Fortress television show? Test your teamwork and experience hours full of fun and adrenaline. TEPfactor is just 40 km from the Prague ( how to reach us ) near the Slap Dam. Forget your usual worries behind your head and step into a completely different world ( check out TEPfactor).

Inside a pirate cave there are 25 games rooms waiting for you. You and teammates (team can have 2-5 players) perform tasks that will test your skill, strength, logic and patience. With every completed challenge you get one of the clues to help you open the four bonus chambers. If you succeed, the treasure chest is waiting for you at the beach. You can perform tasks in any order, the number of attempts is not limeited and there is no time limit. The regular time of the game is 3-4 hours.

TEPfactor is for anyone who wants to experience adventure and fun. Whether you're looking for a weekend program or planning a teambuilding or a school trip, you're in the right place.
In addition, you do not have to deal with the weather - we have summer for the whole year under the roof of TEPfactor.
